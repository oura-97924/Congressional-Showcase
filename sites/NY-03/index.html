The App
What We Built
Bridging Healthcare Gaps Together

HealthBridge is a multilingual platform connecting uninsured and underinsured people with affordable healthcare resources. Millions of low-income and non-English-speaking families struggle to access care due to a lack of guidance on how to reach it. HealthBridge addresses this critical gap with an AI-powered chat assistant, a searchable clinic database, an insurance coverage quiz, and resources in 18+ languages. Available as an iOS app and a web platform.

Features
What HealthBridge Does
Clinic Finder
3,000+ healthcare facilities searchable by state, specialty, insurance, and language
AI Healthcare Chat
AI-powered assistant that searches clinics, insurance providers, and the web in plain language
Coverage Quiz
Personalized insurance recommendations based on income, household size, state, and age
Insurance Directory
55+ providers with searchable plans, cost comparisons, and low-income options
Native iOS App
SwiftUI app with full offline mode, push notifications, and in-app donations
18 Languages
Every page and feature fully translated, including English, Spanish, Arabic, Chinese, Hindi, and more
