About Me

I am a high school student bridging the gap between Medicine and Computer Science. HeartAngel is the result of my mission to make life-saving response accessible to everyone.

The Vision

Giving Life Another Chance.

A mobile tool providing real-time CPR guidance and connecting bystanders to the nearest AED devices.

Flutter / Dart

Innovation

HeartAngel uses real-time haptic and visual feedback to ensure CPR depth and speed are accurate. This bridges the gap of bystander unreadiness, ensuring that anyone can save a life during a cardiac emergency.
